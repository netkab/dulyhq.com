Are you ready for Agentic Commerce?

By 2030, agentic commerce is projected to drive up to $5T globally. As transactions shift from "Human-to-System" to "Agent-to-Agent," the rules of engagement are being rewritten.

The Challenge

Intermediation Risk

When AI agents take over the "intent and execution" of a purchase, merchants risk losing their most valuable assets:

  • Direct Relationship: The agent becomes the "face" of the transaction.
  • Commercial Levers: Bundles, upsells, and loyalty triggers vanish.
  • Brand Trust: Diluted attribution as agents abstract the checkout.

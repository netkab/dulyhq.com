Minoris – The Agentic Risk Platform

AI-native intelligence for modern fraud ecosystems

Market Shift

Generative AI has industrialized deception: deepfakes, voice cloning, and autonomous probing have become cheap and ubiquitous, collapsing traditional verification and driving a surge in AI-driven attacks.

Fraud prevention is shifting from a niche function into critical infrastructure, with the category expanding from ~$15B today toward $65B+ by the early 2030s.

The Systemic Problem

Fraud has become a fast-moving, adaptive system — but the defenses protecting it haven’t kept up.

Most companies think they “use AI” because they run a fraud-scoring model, yet everything around that model is manual and siloed: rule tuning, RCA, drift investigation, vendor monitoring, calibrations, and impact analysis all rely on analysts clicking through disconnected dashboards.

This gap is widening fast. Attackers use AI to probe and adapt at machine speed — and the rise of agentic commerce only amplifies the complexity.

What Minoris Does
  • Always-On Risk Operator – detects drift, decay, vendor degradation, and multi-step failures
